Our Story

Born from the Banks of the Brahmaputra

Beki is named after the gentle river that winds through the Brahmaputra valley — a symbol of life, flow, and deep cultural roots. We are an Assamese kitchen and boutique dedicated to sharing the warmth of our land with the world.

Our restaurant serves traditional Assamese recipes passed down through generations — slow-cooked, thoughtfully spiced, and served with love. Our boutique celebrates the artisans of Assam through hand-woven Mekhela Sadors and authentic Kaahor (bell metal) utensils.

Every dish, every fabric, every utensil here carries a story from the Northeast.
